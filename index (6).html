What is a Microprocessor?

Microprocessor vs Microcontroller

A microprocessor is an IC that functions as a complete CPU on a single chip. It processes data by executing programmed instructions and managing all computational & control tasks.

Unlike a microcontroller, it does not include memory, I/O, or peripherals on the same chip â€” it needs external components.
